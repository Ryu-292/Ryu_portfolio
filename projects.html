NeuroAesthetic

NeuroAesthetic

Imagine sitting in a room filled with soothing scent of incense. As you breath in the aroma, your brain tell it story, not in words but in colors, patterns and shapes.
This project is an exploration of how brainwaves and incense work together to foster self expression and mental well being.

Elastup

Elastup

ElastUp is a multipurpose cutlery holder designed for lunch boxes, combining functionality with a touch of elegance. Planned for launch on April 03, 2025, via Kickstarter, this innovative product features a durable elastic band paired with a piece of leather, offering a minimalist solution for organizing utensils on the go.
In addition to its primary use, ElastUp can also be used on iPads or notebooks to hold pens, making it a versatile accessory
