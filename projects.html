Projects

"How to Make Almost Anything" offered me hands-on experience in creating a wide range of projects. It covered practical skills like using 3D printers, cutting machines, and soldering electronic components onto circuit boards. The hands-on approach allowed me to bring my ideas to life, helping me develop both creative and technical abilities as I built functional prototypes from scratch.

NeuroAesthetic

NeuroAesthetic

Imagine sitting in a room filled with smoothing scent of incense. As you breath in the aroma, your brain tell it story, not in words but in colors, patterns and shapes.
This project is an exploration of how brainwaves and incense work together to foster self expression and mental well being.
